A short history

Originally a project targeted at learners, neoan3 was first used in production roughly a decade ago. Since then, industry standards, performance needs, testability, continuous integration, scalability, maintainability and reusability became more and more relevant in ongoing development.

Used internally by teams inside the corporate world, neoan3 now moves inside the open source world and is looking forward to users, contributors and patrons.

But what is it?

Neoan3 is not only another all purpose framework; its focus is speed and security.

What we mean by speed

Neoan3 is rapidly fast in the following ways:

  • Learning & Onboarding (You can be productive in neoan3 within hours)
  • Development (scaffolding, templating, transforming and a powerful cli helper)
  • Integration Cycle (setup for unit & ci testing)
  • Delivery (fastest benchmark compared to all major frameworks)

What we mean by secure

Neoan3 has many built-in security layers

  • It's impossible to create insecure database transactions
  • It ships with Json Web Token restrictions
  • Easy and secure CORS policy
  • Sandboxed core
